About

I am a PGRS member of the Physics Education research group in the School of Physical Sciences at The Open University. I use a large datset of all undergraduate students in UK higher education (2012/13 to 2019/20) to investigate inequity in physics degree outcomes and how these inequalities relate to those in other subjects.
